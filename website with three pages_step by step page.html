Steps:


1. Provide a Context

Providing a context allows chatGPT to provide your querries with answers exactly how you want it.
Our friend is so smart that if you don't provide such a context it will give you probably too much
than you are looking for. Here is an example: "multiply 643.82893923 by 484.48394893 and leave the
answer in three decimal places". So in our example above our context is the part that says "leave the
answer in three decimal places". The remaining 5 decimal places will be rounded of and, then we get
answer like this 311,924.77


2. Give it Examples

let's understand this by expanding our example above."Multiply 643.82893923 by 484.48394893 and leave the
answer in three decimal places. Example: 22.1111 multiplied by 33.2222 = 734.58". The example provides a
format that our super ai should present us with our answer


3. Refine your Results

lets assume you wanted the results to be in two decimal places but you didn't want it rounded off. You can
just simply refine the results by putting in another querry like this: "thanks but just leave it in two
decimal places without rounding it off". So the 732.58 result it gave you will be changed to 732.57


All that was super easy right! Now you know how to get the best out of ChatGPT. Cheers!
